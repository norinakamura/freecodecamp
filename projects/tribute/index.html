Mahandas(Mahatma Gandhi

)

The leader of the Indian independence movement in Bristish ruled India

Here's a time line of Mahandas Gandhi's life:

  • 1869 - born october 2 in Porbandar, India.
  • 1891 - set up law practice in Bombay, but with little success, then accepted a position with an Indian firm in South Africa.
  • 1906 - Gandhi led a campaign of civil disobedience that last eight years.
  • 1919 - Gandhi launched an organized campaign of passive resistance to Parliament's passage of Rowlatt Acts.
  • 1921 - Gandhi took leadership of the congress.
  • 1930 - he launched a new civil disobedience against the colonial government's tax on salt.
  • 1934 - Gandhi announcec his retirement from politics.
  • 1948 - dies at the age of 78.
